BBa_K5283028 USP45 DNA 81bp USP45, full name Ubiquitin Specific Peptidase 45, is a deubiquitinase located in the cytoplasm and nucleus. It belongs to the ubiquitin-specific processing protease (USP) subfamily, members of which remove ubiquitin tags from substrate proteins by hydrolysis of ubiquitin chains or individual ubiquitin molecules.
